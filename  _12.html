Story Summary:

Karen Berg is Vice President of Operations and Business Strategy at Contoso, a retail manufacturing company specializing in electronics. In this role, she is responsible for overseeing Contoso’s operations and processes to ensure they support the growth and strategic direction of the company.

 

Karen begins her day by reviewing her email in Outlook 2016. She uses helpful Outlook apps, sends a confidential message and easily follows Contoso’s security policies. Lastly, she reviews an email about a presales issue that she wants to investigate. She sets up a virtual meeting with Contoso stores and resellers for this afternoon to share the results of her pending investigation as well as a new plan to course correct.

 

For participants interested in using Outlook apps, instruct them to:

1.From the desktop, type Outlook from the Search bar.

2.Within Outlook 2016, navigate to left menu and click Clutter

3.Back in Inbox, scroll through to find an email from Amy Alberts titled Contoso’s Annual Meeting – Happy Hour!

4.Click on this email.

5.Within the email, click on Bing Maps to quickly view the event's location.

6.Next, click on Action Items within email.

7.Click on Follow up to flag this email as a reminder to RSVP.

 

To send a confidential email that cannot be forwarded, instruct participants to do the following:

1.In Outlook 2016, click +New in the top ribbon to create a new email.

2.Type TyC@tenantname.onmicrosoft.com in To: line.

3.Click Check names from the ribbon.

4.Type Confidential in the subject line.

5.Type message in body of email.

6.Click Send.

 

To experience a DLP policy, instruct participants to do the following:

1.In Outlook 2016, click +New in the top ribbon to create a new email.

2.Type BobH@proseware.com in To: line.

3.Click Check names from the ribbon.

4.In email body, type message that includes the following:

Visa CC# 4111-1111-1111-1111 

Expires: 08/2018 

1.When it appears, view Policy Tip stating the message can't be sent because it appears to contain sensitive information.

2.Click Discard to delete the email draft.

 

For participants interested in setting up a Skype for Business meeting with Modern Attachments in Outlook 2016, instruct them to do the following:

1.Scroll to find an email from Carlos titled M400 Smartphone Launch Concerns.

2.Click on this email.

3.Click on Meeting in the Respond section of the ribbon. 

4.Click Skype meeting, then Add Skype meeting.

5.Click Insert > Attach File and select OneDrive - Contoso. 

6.Double-clicks the Store Visit Notebook folder. 

7.Click Open Notebook.onetoc2.

8.Click Insert. 

9.Click the arrow on the attached file. 

10.Click Recipients can view in the Change permissions drop-down menu. 

11.Add the Operations and Business Strategy and Sales distribution lists to the Attendees: field. 

12.Fill in the Event details. 

13.Add a quick message in the body of the email to add context for the meeting

14.Select a date and time. 

15.Click Send.
