What's New

The HubOne Kairos Immersion Experience (KIE) provides a facilitated hands-on environment where practices get an introduction to Windows and the new Office and provides an opportunity to experience these powerful new productivity solutions coupled with the power of OnePractice. Customers are able to experience Microsoft technologies including Office 365 cloud services like Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, Skype for Business, Yammer, OneDrive for Business, Delve, Power BI as well as the Enterprise Mobility Suite, Windows, Windows Phone, and Dynamics CRM. KIE sessions are designed to be interactive, experiential, and fun.

 

A KIE is not a click-path demonstration over all of the features the products offer. It’s a true-to-life user experience that takes participants through common work-related scenarios such as being productive while mobile, using social networking to get work done, and connecting in real time with coworkers and customers. KIE gives you a first-hand look at the fast and fluid experience of Windows 10 and the exciting features of the new Office applications and services across a variety of devices, including tablets, PCs, and smartphones. Participating in a KIE is the best way to learn about business productivity tools and technologies without sales pressure.

 

 

The KIE is a great tool in your arsenal to:

•Build excitement about Windows 10 and the new Office

•Allow customers to explore solutions/products at their own pace in a non-threatening, risk-free environment

•Re-engineer the customer-buying vision by taking the conversation from a product-specific discussion to a platform discussion

 

 

In particular, schedule a KIE for your customers when:

 

•It is time for their Microsoft EA or updated EA renewal

•The customer is interested in the purchase of a new EA

•The customer is evaluating switching to a competitor (compete situation)

•The customer has rights to but hasn’t deployed the latest Microsoft products
