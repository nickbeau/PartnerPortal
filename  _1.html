Capture, review and share notes from anywhere

Facilitation Tip: All personas will have their own Store Visits Notebook in their OneDrive account for use during the session.

 

While eating her breakfast at the coffee shop, Karen easily transitions to her tablet to continue working.

 

 
From the Windows 10 lock screen, Karen swipes up and types in her password: pass@word1.

 

 

To access her online notebook, she navigates to portal.microsoftonline.com and signs in with her credentials: karenb@<yourtenantname>.onmicrosoft.com and pass@word1…

 

 

and clicks OneDrive in the Office 365 App Switcher.

 

 

 

Facilitation Tip:

OneDrive for Business allows users to store work documents for easy access and sharing across Windows devices. If something happens to the device, the documents stored in OneDrive for Business are still safe and accessible.

 

Facilitation Tip:

Click Open in OneNote to use inking with the Surface Pen.

 

Competitive Advantage:

OneDrive for Business offers 1TB of storage per user as part of every Office 365 subscription.
 

 

She then clicks on Store Visit Notebook.

 

 

She clicks Open in OneNote.

 

 

She creates a new page with today's date and adds her meeting notes along with a list of things she wants to do when she gets back in the office.

 

 

When finished, she navigates back to her Internet browser and clicks the sharing icon next to the notebook to securely share it with her team so they can review her notes and quickly get up to speed if Karen needs their assistance.

 

 

She clicks Invite people and adds her colleagues names in the first field before typing a brief message:

 

Got some interesting feedback from one of Contoso’s store managers today regarding the M400; please take a look as I will be researching the issue further and may need your assistance.

Thanks!
 

She clicks Share to share the notebook with the team.

 

 

After meeting with the store manager, Karen would like to create a PowerPoint so she and her team can start can putting together a presentation addressing some of the M400 Presale issues.

 

Karen clicks New, and select PowerPoint presentation from the list.

 

 

Karen wants to have the PowerPoint ready in her OneDrive so she and her team can work on it later. She quickly adds a title to the presentation and selects a theme using the PowerPoint Online.

 

Karen clicks on her name to return to her OneDrive account.

 

Lastly, she selects the presentation and clicks rename. She renames the presentation M400 Concerns and clicks Save.

 

 

It's almost time for Karen to take the bus back to Contoso, but she wants to continue working. Since the bus doesn't have Wi-Fi, Karen decides to sync OneDrive to her tablet so she can access the files offline.

 

Switching back to the original browser tab titled Files - OneDrive and then tap or click into the blank white space of the page before she taps or clicks Sync above the document list.

 

 

She taps or clicks Sync now and then Sync Now again…

 

 

 

 

 

and taps or clicks Show my files…, which takes her into the desktop File Explorer.

 

 

 

 

With files synced to her device for offline access, Karen gathers her things and walks to the bus stop as it's pulling up to the curb.
