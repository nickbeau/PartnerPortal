Mobile Productivity

Facilitation Tip: If you are using an Android or iOS device to showcase this section, please review the setup steps available in the Technical Setup Guide. Please be sure to read through the guide and complete all steps prior to your session.

 

To get directions on a Windows Phone 8.1:

Swipe down to review the Action Center.

Tap the magnifying glass icon.

Ask Cortana for directions to the nearest coffee shop.

Tap Directions on the first coffee shop.

View directions on the Map app.

 

To set a time-based reminder with Cortana:

Tap search icon.

Ask Cortana to set a reminder to send the store manager an update  before she leaves the office at 5 p.m. today.

 

To view and send email on a Windows Phone 8.1:

Swipe up to enter the Start screen.

Tap and hold the Outlook tile.

Tap the arrow to expand.

Tap the Outlook tile.

Swipe right to filter to urgent emails only.

Tap the email titled Needs Review: Final Presentation

Open the PowerPoint attachment.

Tap through the slides of the presentation.
Tap respond, then reply on the email.
Type message.
Tap send.

 

To move an email on a Windows Phone 8.1:

Tap on the ellipsis.

Tap move.

Select the Conferences folder.

 

To flag an email on a Windows Phone 8.1:

Find the Litigation Meeting email.

Tap the ellipsis.

Tap the toggle flag for follow-up.

 

To flag multiple messages:

Select the checkbox to the left of each message.

Click set flag.
