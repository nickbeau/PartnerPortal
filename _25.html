Sales Productivity

 

 

To track emails in Dynamics CRM:

Navigate to the Internet browser.

Click on Mail from the Office 365 App Switcher.

Click on the email from Alex Wu.

Click on Dynamics CRM at the top of the email.

Click on Track.

Click on the Northwind Printers opportunity name in the email.

 

To easily up-sell and cross-sell items through CRM:

Select Product Line Items.

Click on the Suggestions link.

Click on Pick to the right of the Digitizer Desktop 3D Scanner.

Click Add to List.

 

To capture deal-specific notes within the CRM record:

Click on OneNote > Team Meeting from within the CRM record.

Add notes.

 

To work with the team on sales pursuits via Office 365 Groups:

Click on the Northwind Printers opportunity in the navigation ribbon.

Click on Office 365 Groups.

Type a request in Office Groups Conversations.

Click Send.

Navigate to CRM from the Internet browser.

Navigate to the Groups Document List.

Open the 3D Printer Product Pitch_v04.pptx file.

Edit the presentation.

 

To view documents from within the CRM Modern App:

Open the CRM for Mobile App

Locate the Northwind Printers opportunity.

Click on the sales proposal and the 3D printer presentations.

 

To connect business data from Dynamics CRM in Cortana:

Click on Cortana.

Navigate to the OneNote notebook.

Click on the Northwind Traders account.

Open the full Active Deals menu.

Scroll to find the Northwind Printers opportunity.

Click on it to open it in the CRM Tablet App.

 

To complete follow-up tasks using After Meeting:

Click on the checkmark icon in the CRM Tablet App.

Select After meeting.

Update tasks with details obtained in the meeting.

 

To use Power BI with CRM:

Navigate to Power BI from the Office 365 App Switcher.

Click on the Sales Manager (Dynamics CRM) dashboard.

Types Won Revenue into the Q&A box.

Type by industry behind won revenue.

Type the word Pie to the Q&A box.

 

To use Immersive Excel with CRM:

Return to CRM.

Click on Sales > Opportunities.

Click on Export to Excel > Open in Excel Online.

Change the forecast amount to $500,000.

Change the rating to Hot.

Change the estimated closed date to next week.

Click on Save Changes to CRM to update her opportunity list.
