Use social tools to find experts and answers

Story Summary:

To begin her investigation into low presales of Contoso’s latest smartphone, Karen wants to look for candid feedback regarding the product from employees across the organization. She decides to review Contoso’s Yammer account, a social hub that offers employees a variety of ways to share their thoughts, concerns and suggestions with the company without being blocked by silos. She then utilizes Delve, IM and video conferencing to reach out quickly to company experts who can help her continue the investigation.

 

For participants interested in utilizing Yammer, instruct them to:

 

1.Click on the Office 365 App menu in the upper left corner.

2.Click on Sites from the menu.

3.Click on Yammer tile. If needed, sign in with credentials.

4.Click on Product Feedback under Groups on the left side.

5.In the Product Feedback Yammer feed, find the post by Sanjay Shah about the M400 phone.

6.Recognize Sanjay's suggestion by clicking Like on his post.

1.View the reply post from Christa Geller.

2.Click on More and choose Follow in Inbox to receive notifications with updates on this conversations.

3.Click on All Company on the left navigation.

4.In the Update field, type a post asking employees to share any feedback they have on the M400.

5.Type Sanjay in the Add people to notify field and select him from the dropdown.

6.Click Add topics.

7.Type and select #M400.  

8.Click Post.

 

 

To show participants how to use Delve and Skype for Business, instruct them to:

1.Click on Office 365 App menu in the upper left corner.

2.Click Delve.

3.Type social in to the Search field.

4.View documents authored by Kelly Krout.

5.Click on Kelly’s name.

6.View Kelly's profile.

7.Click on the Message icon in Kelly's profile.

8.Type and send message.

9.Drag VP of Corporate Marketing Alan Steiner, VP of Sales Operations Julian Isla and Director of Sales Analysis Molly Clark in to the Skype for Business message window.

10.Click the camera icon to escalate to video/audio call.
