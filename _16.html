Coauthor and share content quickly

Story Summary:

After learning the product’s marketing messages are underperforming, Karen and her team decide to roll out new messages to course correct. Together, they update the product’s marketing collateral simultaneously with improved messages based on their research. They are now ready to share their findings and updated collateral at the afternoon meeting.
 

To show participants how to coauthor a PowerPoint presentation, instruct them to do the following:

1.From the intranet Home page, click Sales & Marketing then click Collateral Center from the left navigation.

2.On the embedded M400 Promotion PowerPoint presentation, click Edit Presentation > Edit in PowerPoint.

3.Click Share icon in the ribbon

4.Add names of personas in the session in the Share window.

5.Click Share; other personas will receive an email in Outlook with a link to the file.

6.Scroll down to slide 3 and make edits while another participant is making changes to slide 4.

7.At the same time, add a slide to the deck by right-clicking in the slide previewer on the left and selecting New Slide.

 

To show participants how they can search the internet without leaving their documents thanks to Smart Lookup, instruct them to:

1.Click the second slide

2.Right-click on the word smartphone.

3.Click Smart Lookup.

4.Click on picture in the Bing Image Search window.

5.Save image to desktop.

 

To show participants how the easily find a desired action with Tell Me, instruct them to:

1.Click Tell me what you want to do…

2.Type Insert Pictures.

3.Click Insert Pictures

4.Select image saved to desktop from Smart Lookup.

5.Click Save to sync changes back to SharePoint.
