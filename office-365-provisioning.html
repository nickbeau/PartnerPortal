Office 365 Provisioning

Important: After your tenant has completed the provisioning process, or you have reset your tenant, verify IRM is activated in your tenant prior to your session. Navigate to Office365 Admin console > Service Settings > Rights Management > Manage to check activation status. If it is not active, simply click the activate button to enable rights management features in your tenant.

 

In advance of your KIE for Office 365 deployment and prior to any client setup, you will need to provision your Office 365 tenant.

To begin, visit www.microsoftcie.com.

Once authenticated, click My Dashboard

Then, click Create Tenant.

There are now three options for provisioning a tenant–using a QuickTenant, provisioning a traditional tenant with a custom name or using an existing tenant.

QuickTenant is a new approach to tenant provisioning that will provide greater stability in the tenant provisioning process by preparing a large inventory of completed tenants available to customers immediately. QuickTenant will mitigate most customer-facing difficulties associated with the provisioning process due to outages and unexpected events in the provisioning engine that prevent successful completion of a tenant. It will also allow for tenants to be claimed by a customer instantly, eliminating the need for extended wait times.

To receive a QuickTenant, click on QuickTenant and click Next.

On the next screen, select your Industry and Language and click Next.

Select a data center (region) and click Finish to receive your QuickTenant.

 

To provision a traditional tenant with a custom name, click on the Custom Tenant tile and click Next.

On the next screen, select your Industry and Language and click Next.

Select your Region and click Next.

Add your Email, Desired Tenant Name, and Phone number and click Next.

 

The Office 365 Signup service will send a verification code to your mobile phone via SMS text message. Enter the code into the Verification Code field and click Finish.

 

To check the status of provisioning, you may return to this page at any time and choose the My Tenants option.

 

Please allow 8-24 hours for provisioning to complete.

Once your tenant completes provisioning, please review the Known Issues section for workarounds to known issues.

Once you have reviewed the known issues, you are ready to move on to Setting up the front end.
