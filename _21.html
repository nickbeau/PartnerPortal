IT Security and Control

 

To use Outlook 2016:

Tap on the Windows icon to open the Start menu.

Type Outlook 2016 in the search bar.

Select Outlook 2016.

Right-click on By Date.

Click Show as Conversations.

Click This folder.

Click email from Carlos Grilo titled Customer Service Improvement.

Click Respond > Forward.

Type a message congratulating Jamie and the team.

Type All in the To: line.

Click Check Names.

Click Send.

Right-click the conversation thread.

Click Ignore.

Click Ignore Conversation.

Click on the email titled Contoso’s Annual Meeting – Happy Hour!

Click on Action Items.

Click the flag on the Litigation Meeting email.

 

To send a confidential email that can't be forwarded:

Click +new.

Type message.

Type TyC@tenantname.onmicrosoft.com in the To: line.

Click Check Names.

Type confidential in the subject line.

Click Send.

 

To experience DLP policy in Outlook 2016:

Click +new.

Type BobH@proseware.com in the To: line.

Click Check Names.

Type message that includes:

Visa CC# 4111-1111-1111-1111 

Expires: 08/2018 

View Policy Tip regarding sensitive information.

Click Discard.

 

To experience DLP policy in OneDrive for Business:

Open the Internet browser.

Click the Office 365 App Switcher.

Click OneDrive.

Find the Contoso Online Presales Report.

Click on the Policy Tip.

Click on Resolve.

Close Resolve Policy Tip box.

Open the Contoso Online Presales Report file.

Select the contents of the spreadsheet.

Copy it into a new Excel file.

Remove the column with credit card data.

Save the file.

 

To share an Excel Notebook:

Click on Share in the upper-right hand corner.

Enter the names of the participants.

Click Share.

 

To discover information in eDiscovery:

Open the Internet browser.

Navigate to portal.microsoftonline.com.

Sign in to the Office 365 portal with the credentials:

karenb@yourtenantname.onmicrosoft.com and pass@word1

Click Sites in the top ribbon.

Click Home under Sites I’m following.

Click Operations.

Click Legal.

Click eDiscovery. 

Click Litigation.

Click on new item listed under Search and Export > Queries.

Click the Name field and type Patent Litigation.

Type the term patent infringement in the Query box.

Click Modify Query Scope.

Click Search Everything.

Select Exchange and SharePoint.  

Click OK.

Click Search.

Click SharePoint.

Click Intranet Home.

 

To start a document workflow:

Click Sales & Marketing.

Click Collateral Center.

Click Collateral Document Library.

Click L270 Campaign.

Click on the ellipsis next to the PowerPoint presentation.

Click on ellipsis > Advanced > Workflows.

Click Start a New Workflow.

Click Marketing Document Approval.

Click Start.

 

To approve a document workflow:

Click on the email from the Collateral Center

Click on the URL at the bottom of the email.

Click on the Open this task link in the email.

Click Approve.
