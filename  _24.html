To use Tell Me to insert a PivotTable:

Select the entire spreadsheet.

Click Tell me what you want to do… in the ribbon.

Type PivotChart.

Select PivotChart & PivotTable from the menu.

Click OK.

Select Reseller and TotalSales.

Drag the Count of TotalSales into the Values area.

Leave Reseller in the Row area.

Resize the chart.

Click on the filter drop down above the reseller name.

Select More Sort Options.

Choose Descending Z to A by Count of Total Sales.

Click OK.
