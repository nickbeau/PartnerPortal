Visualize and analyze complex data

Story Summary:

Karen and her teammates decide to research sales numbers and product perception data so they use self-service BI tools, like natural language query and 3D visualizations. They discover the low sales may be due to negative social media sentiment caused by poor marketing messages.

 

For participants interested in reviewing data utilizing Featured Reports in Power BI, instruct them to:

1.Click on the Office 365 App menu in the upper left corner.

1.Click on Power BI tile.

1.Click Sign In.

1.Click on Smart Phone Analysis in Reports menu.

2.Choose View.

3.Click Presale filter under Promotion Type to compare M300 and M400 sales during Presale timeline.

4.Click the funnel icon with the red x to clear all filters to see how sales for the M300 performed after the presale period.

 

For participants interested in Featured Questions and natural language query/Q&A, instruct them to:

1.Click on Smart Phone Social Sentiment dashboard.

2.In the field at the top of the window, type Show sentiment phrase and number of m400 tweets

1.View results.

1.Next, type Show average tweet sentiment value as a line chart during presale by month for M400.

2.View results.

1.Type Show number of M400 tweets as a line chart during presale by month.

View results.

1.Type Show number of negative tweets for M400 in only the United States by state and zoom in to see results.

 

For participants interested in using Power View and 3D Maps, instruct them to:

1.Click the ellipsis next to the M400 Power Map for Smart Phone Twitter report.

1.Choose View.

1.Click Edit Workbook>Edit in Excel.

2.Click the Edit Workbook and Enable Content buttons and accept it as a trusted document.

3.Click in the Insert tab in the ribbon.

4.Click on the down arrow on the 3D map button.

5.Click Open 3D Maps.

6.Click on Open for the Northeast M400 Presales tour.

7.Click Play Tour in the upper left corner of the Home Menu Ribbon.
