Once you have  subscribed to CA Kairos using the standard package Online Order form, the following process occurs: -

1.Your credit card is charged. CA Kairos is billed per user monthly, in advance, although you can cancel at any time

2.A confirmation email is sent to HubOne who will commence the fulfilment process

3.Your Office 365 tenancy is ordered and provisioned with Microsoft

4.A license is granted for the Connected Practice Applications

5.The Connected Practice Applications are installed and configured

6.You are sent a login by Microsoft

7.An Azure tenancy is provisioned under the same login
