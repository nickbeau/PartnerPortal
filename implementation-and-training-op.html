Implementation and Training Opportunities

As well as the service itself there are additional opportunities for you to generate revenue.

 

•Document Migration – HubOne’s Document Migration tool has been used to migrate many Tb of data from servers to the cloud and partners get unlimited access to this tool to migrate documents for your clients.

•OnePractice Portal Installation and Configuration – Our automated tools streamline the configuration of SharePoint and our tools, meaning delivery is a wizard approach.

•OnePractice Portal Training (Champion and Team) – We can deliver Train the Trainer or train your customer if that’s what you require.

•Office 365 Migration – We can consult on the Seamless migration of calendars, contacts, emails, domains and more

•Xero Practice Manager Implementation & Training – We can provide data import from common practice management systems to Xero Practice Manager; Integration with Xero; Provide job, quote, invoice and lead templates; 1 on 1 training with nominated champion and training for the customers team; 1 hour post implementation review.
