Coauthor and share content easily

Facilitation Tip: If you are using an Android or iOS device to showcase this section, please review the setup steps available in the Technical Setup Guide. Please be sure to read through the guide and complete all steps prior to your session.

 

 

The team agrees to review and edit the M400 marketing messages together so they all navigate to the Contoso intranet through the Office 365 portal.

 

They sign in to portal.microsoftonline.com with their credentials.

 

 

They click Sites in top ribbon, then Home under Sites I’m following.

 

 

 

Next, they visit the Collateral Center by clicking Sales & Marketing > Collateral Center and find the M400_Promotion PowerPoint presentation in the PPT Slide Library at the bottom of the page.

 

 

 

They click Edit Presentation > Edit in PowerPoint Online to edit it in their browser.

 

 

With coauthoring in PowerPoint Online, each team member can view and edit the document at the same time from any web browser, giving them the flexibility to be productive and collaborative from a multitude of devices and locations.

 

Kelly edits slide 3 while Karen makes changes to slide 4.

 

 

 

Now that they are finished, Karen clicks the Share link. She adds the Resellers distribution list and clicks Share.

 

 

Karen knows that anyone with a browser can open, view and edit the PowerPoint using Office Online.
