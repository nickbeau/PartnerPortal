Click on the PowerPoint file  from the email.

Go to the second slide.

Go to the Drawing Tools/Format ribbon.

Click the square icon in the Shapes drop down.

Change the Shape Fill to No Fill.

Change the Shape Outline to red.

Resize the box so it encompasses the last bulleted text.

To create a Skype Meeting Broadcast:

Open a new tab in the web browser.

Go to portal.broadcast.skype.com and sign in, if required.

Click New meeting.

Fill out the meeting details, like meeting title, time, and date.

Set the meeting access to Anonymous.

Click Done.

Click Show.

Copy the link.
