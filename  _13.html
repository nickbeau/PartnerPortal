Capture, review and share notes from anywhere

 

Story Summary:

 

Karen begins her investigation of the presales issue by accessing an online notebook in which all her colleagues will be able to collaborate with her no matter where they are.

 

Facilitation Tip: All personas will have their own Store Visits Notebook in their OneDrive account for use during the session.

 

To show participants how to capture, review and share notes, instruct them to:

1.From a browser, navigate to portal.microsoftonline.com and sign in with credentials: persona@yourtenantname.onmicrosoft.com and pass@word1

2.Click on the Office 365 App menu (grid-shaped icon) in the upper left corner of the window.

3.Click OneDrive in the Office 365 App menu.

4.Click on Store Visit Notebook.

5.Click +Page to create a new page with today's date and type notes.

a.Note: Click Open in OneNote to use inking with the Surface Pen.

6.When finished typing notes, click on Karen's name on the left side of the top of the window to navigate back to OneDrive.

7.Click the sharing icon (people or lock icon) next to the notebook.

8.In the pop-up window, click Invite people.

9.Add names of other personas in the session in the Enter names or email addresses… field.

10.Type message in the Include a personal message with this invitation (Optional) field.

11.Click Share.

 

 

If participants are interested in syncing the online notebook to local device for offline access, instruct them to:

 

12.Click Sync above the document list.

13.From the pop-up windows, click Sync now, Yes and then Sync Now again…

14.To see the files on the local device, click Show my files…, which takes participants into the desktop File Explorer.
