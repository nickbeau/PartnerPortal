Add work account and Sharepoint mapped drive

1.Windows menu

2.Settings

3.Accounts

4.Work Access

5.Connect

6.Enter your Office 365 credentials (email address and password) and follow instructions

7.Open Internet Explorer > go to https://portal.office.com if it ask for your credentials, please tick "keep me signed in"

8.Choose "Sites" > "Team Sites"

9.On the left pane, find the folder you want to map

10. Click on the little drop down arrow just once

11.On the top left corner, click on LIBRARY

12.In the ribbon, click on "open with Explorer"

13.Click in blank space of Explorer's address bar to highlight it

14.Right click on highlighted text

15.Copy

16.On the left pane, click on "This PC"

17.On the top pane, click on "Map Network drive"

18.Choose any letter you prefer

19.Underneath, right click > past

20.Finish

21.Reboot your PC and Test
