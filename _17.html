Identity & Access Management

 

 

 

                                                                                                                                                                                       Identity + Access Management

 

With Azure Active Directory, Contoso is able to centrally manage identities across its data center and the cloud and provide employees with services, such as secure single sign-on to all of their applications and self-service.

 

Karen can manage her profile and devices easily in Microsoft Azure's MyApps center. She opens a browser, navigates to http://myapps.microsoft.com, and signs in with her username and password.

 

 

She can then easily navigate to any Contoso cloud app available to her.

 

 

She also can change her password without submitting a request to the IT department. In the Microsoft Azure MyApps center, she clicks on Profile and then on the Change password tile.

 

 

She enters her current password of pass@word1 as the old password, enters pass@word2 as her new password, and clicks Submit.

 

 

With Azure Active Directory, corporate users can sign into all of their applications once with one ID and password, regardless if the apps are on-premises, in the cloud or a combination of both. Azure Active Directory can also be configured to provide self-service password resets, group, and device management all from the MyApps center.

 

Thanks to Azure Active Directory, Karen is able to be more productive throughout her day without relying on the IT department for assistance.
